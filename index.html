Currently, Amir is a Researcher at Data Semantics Lab Research Center Kansas State University, Manhattan, KS. He is broadly interested in Data Science, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Statistical Analysis, Inference, Data Analytics, Hypothesis Testing, Knowledge Graph, Knowledge Engineering, NLP, Information Extraction, Medical Informatics, and Big data. His research applies multidisciplinary methods to extract insights of data.

Previously, he worked as a data/research scientist at Weill Cornell Medicine (Cornell University), National Institue of Health (NIH), and Information Science Institute(ISI).

About Data Semantics (DaSe) Laboratory

Data Semantics (DaSe) Laboratory is research lab undertaking research in applied and theoretical topics in the broad areas of Artificial Intelligence and/or Data Science. It brings together researchers from Kansas State University, and industry in the pursuit of knowledge and applications related to artificial intelligence and data science Kansas State University that performs world-class research on translating information to meaning.
